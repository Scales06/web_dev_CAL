About Sofia Martinez

Hi, my name is Sofia Martinez and I am a HUGE ANIME FANATIC,
hence why I'm going to talk about why I have decided to open a storefront, an anime storefront to be exact.

As I said before, I absolutely LOVE ANIME, and so it only makes sense that I'd open up my own anime shop.
My hope with this website is to spread the joy of anime to newcomers and anime veterans alike!

On my website, there will be lots of content to meet the expectations of anime fans such as myself.
For example:

  1. Products and Information Sector
  2. Customer FAQ
  3. Events and Upcoming Events
  4. Offers and Discounts

My inspirations

The idea behind this storefront is influenced by various other websites, such as

  • Crunchyroll
  • Forbidden Planet
  • Shueisha

These anime websites and shops brought inspiration to me - who once was a fan that enjoyed watching anime in the comfort of their home
- and now is the proud creator and owner of an entire anime storefront!

Of course, I didn't do this alone. Through the help of talented programmers, I brought my idea to life!
With this storefront, I hope that I can inspire others to create their own ideal websites too!
